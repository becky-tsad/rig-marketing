Our Services

Fencing, decks & gates — built to last

From a single backyard panel to a full agricultural perimeter, we plan it, build it, and stand behind it for five years.

Wood Fencing

Cedar, treated, horizontal, ranch, and privacy styles — proper post depth, level top rail, fences that look square for decades.

Vinyl & Ornamental

Maintenance-free vinyl panels and powder-coated ornamental iron — clean sight lines, no painting, no staining, no fuss.

Chain Link & Commercial

Residential dog runs to large commercial perimeters — galvanized and vinyl-coated chain link installed clean and tensioned right.

Decks

Custom wood, vinyl, and composite decks — engineered for Alberta freeze-thaw, permits handled, finished to match your house.

Automated Gates

Cantilever and swing gates with motors, keypads, and remotes — for driveways, acreages, and commercial yards across the foothills.

Agricultural Fencing

Post-and-wire, post-and-plank, game fence, corrals, and paddocks — built for working acreages, not weekend hobby use.
