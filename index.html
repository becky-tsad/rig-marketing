Questions? I've got answers.

What if I don't have any content or photos?

No problem. I'll pull your best Google reviews, grab your service list from your existing profiles, and source professional stock photos if needed. Most clients don't need to write a single word.

What if I already have a website but it's outdated?

That's most of my clients. I'll rebuild it from scratch with a fresh design, migrate anything worth keeping, and handle the switchover so there's no downtime.

Do I need to know anything technical?

Nope. I handle hosting, domains, SSL certificates, backups — all of it. You just need to know how to check your email (that's where your leads will go).

What's included in the monthly fee?

Hosting, security updates, daily backups, SSL certificate, and small content updates (think: changing your phone number, adding a new service, swapping a photo). Basically, everything to keep your site running without you thinking about it.

What if I need changes after the site launches?

Small updates are included in your monthly fee. If you want a bigger change (new page, new feature), I'll quote it separately — but most clients never need anything beyond what's included.

Can you help with Google Ads or social media?

Right now I'm focused on websites. I may expand to other services down the road, but I'd rather do one thing really well than offer everything and do it halfway.

What if I don't like the design?

You'll see a draft before we launch, and you get one round of revisions included. I've never had a client who wasn't happy with the final result — but if we're truly not a fit, I'll refund your deposit. No hard feelings.

How do I pay?

E-transfer. 50% when you sign the contract, 50% when your site launches. Simple.
